Hi, I'm Mehrnaz.

Software developer. Interior Designer. Art Lover. traveller. foodie. Animal Lover.
I was born in Tehran and I grown up there also fall in love and married in Tehran, Now we live in Vancouver.
My husband and I have a cute dog whose name is Happy because He is always happy.
